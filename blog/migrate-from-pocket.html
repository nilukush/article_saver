Frequently Asked Questions

Will I lose any articles during migration?

No! Article Saver imports 100% of your Pocket library, including archived articles. We've successfully tested with libraries containing over 15,000 articles.

Are my tags preserved?

Yes, all your tags are imported exactly as they were in Pocket. You can continue organizing your articles the same way.

Can I still access my articles offline?

Absolutely! Article Saver downloads the full content of your articles for offline reading, just like Pocket did.

What happens to my Pocket account after import?

Your Pocket account remains unchanged. Article Saver only reads your data; it doesn't modify or delete anything from Pocket.

Can I import multiple times?

Yes! You can re-import anytime to sync new articles you've saved to Pocket. Article Saver intelligently avoids creating duplicates.
